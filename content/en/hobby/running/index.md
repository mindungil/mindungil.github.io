## 소개

I think it’s been about two years since I’ve been running consistently.

It started in the military’s fitness center.

At the time, I was interested in getting rid of body fat and building physical strength.

At first, the fatigue from running was very difficult, and I suffered from muscle pain rather than feeling the effects of the exercise.

But less than a week later, I felt the effects of running.

From then on, I started running once a week.
